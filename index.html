Workflow is the key.

The application focuses on the principle of "clean inbox" - made popular with Google Inbox. When coupling this with known productivity philosophies like Getting Things Done (GTD), things become a breeze to manage.

  • strive to keep Today empty
  • enter tasks that will take > 2 minutes immediately
  • postpone tasks that are less important
  • always define clear and tangible goals
  • focus only on the next step
